## 📌 Introduction
Understanding customer shopping behavior is critical for businesses aiming to optimize sales, enhance customer experience, and drive strategic decision-making.  

This project analyzes transactional and behavioral data to uncover patterns, trends, and actionable insights that influence customer purchasing decisions.

---

## 💼 Business Problem
Retail businesses often struggle to:
- Identify high-value customers  
- Understand purchasing patterns  
- Optimize product placement and marketing strategies  
- Reduce churn and increase customer retention  

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer purchase patterns  
- Segment customers based on behavior  
- Identify top-performing products/categories  
- Discover seasonal and trend-based insights  
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## 🔑 Key Insights

📌 **Top Customers Drive Revenue**  
A small percentage of customers contribute to the majority of sales.

📌 **Seasonal Trends Matter**  
Sales increase significantly during holidays and promotional periods.

📌 **Category Performance**  
Specific categories consistently outperform others.

📌 **Customer Retention Opportunity**  
Repeat purchase behavior indicates strong retention potential.
